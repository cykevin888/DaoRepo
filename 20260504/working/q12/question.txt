Question

The city bus stations are located at equal distances (unit distance)
from each other along a straight road.

Each station has a unique station ID.

The buses do not travel to all of the bus stations. The highway
administration needs to determine the total distance that the buses
cover.

Given the IDs of the bus stations that have a bus operating between
them, write an algorithm to help the administration find the total
distance covered by all the city buses.

Input

1. The first line of the input consists of two space-separated integers
   `num` and `constM`, representing the number of buses (`N`).
   `constM` is always `2`.

2. The next `N` lines each consist of `constM` space-separated integers:
   `busStop1` and `busStop2`, representing the IDs of the bus stations
   that have a bus operating between them.

Output

Print an integer representing the distance covered by the buses.

Constraints

- `0 <= num <= 10^5`
- `1 <= busStop1 < busStop2 <= 10^6`

Example

Input:
3 2
2 4
3 5
6 7

Output:
4

Explanation:

The covered road segments are:

- bus `2 -> 4` covers distance `2`
- bus `3 -> 5` overlaps partially and adds only `1` new unit
- bus `6 -> 7` adds `1` more unit

So the total covered distance is:
`4`

The stations on the road can be viewed as:
`1 - 2 - 3 - 4 - 5 - 6 - 7`

Chinese Translation

题目

城市公交站点位于一条笔直道路上，并且相邻站点之间的距离都相等
（单位距离为 `1`）。

每个站点都有唯一的站点编号。

公交车并不会覆盖所有站点。公路管理部门需要计算所有公交线路总共覆盖
了多长的距离。

给定若干对站点编号，每一对表示有一辆公交车在这两个站点之间运行。
请设计算法，求所有公交车一共覆盖的总距离。

输入

1. 第一行输入两个空格分隔的整数 `num` 和 `constM`，
   其中 `num` 表示公交线路的数量 `N`，`constM` 恒为 `2`。

2. 接下来的 `N` 行中，每行包含两个空格分隔的整数：
   `busStop1` 和 `busStop2`，表示一辆公交车运行的两个站点编号。

输出

输出一个整数，表示所有公交车覆盖的总距离。

约束

- `0 <= num <= 10^5`
- `1 <= busStop1 < busStop2 <= 10^6`

样例

输入：
3 2
2 4
3 5
6 7

输出：
4

解释：

把每辆公交车看成覆盖道路上的一个区间：

- `2 -> 4` 覆盖长度 `2`
- `3 -> 5` 与前一个区间有重叠，因此只新增 `1` 的长度
- `6 -> 7` 再新增 `1` 的长度

所以总覆盖距离为：
`4`
